Arlington is the entertainment capital of the DFW Metroplex. Home to AT&T Stadium — one of the largest stadiums in the world — Globe Life Field, Six Flags, and the area's biggest concert venues, Arlington generates thousands of rides every week. And yet, there's no good public transit, parking is expensive, and rideshare surges on game days can be brutal.


Synergy Lux Limo is the answer. Our premium black car service to Arlington means flat rates — locked at booking, never subject to game day surge pricing. We serve all of Arlington and surrounding cities: Grand Prairie, Mansfield, Irving, DFW Airport area, Fort Worth, and Dallas. Every ride is owner-operated — W. Rodriguez personally behind the wheel, every time.
