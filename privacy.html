Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2026  ·  Synergy Lux Limo DFW LLC

Introduction

Synergy Lux Limo DFW LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a family-owned and operated private chauffeur service based in Dallas–Fort Worth, Texas. We take your privacy seriously and believe you deserve to know exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

This policy applies to information collected through our website (synergyluxlimodfw.com), our booking forms, our AI concierge Amirah, and any SMS or voice communications you have with us.

We don't sell your data. We don't use advertising trackers. We collect only what we need to deliver your ride and communicate with you about it.

Information We Collect

When you request a quote, submit a booking, or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Identity: Your full name
  • Contact: Phone number and email address
  • Trip details: Pickup location, drop-off location, date, time, and number of passengers
  • Payment: Billing information processed securely through Stripe — we never see or store your card number
  • Communications: Chat messages, SMS exchanges, and voice call transcripts through our Amirah AI concierge
  • Usage: Basic analytics about how you interact with our website (pages visited, browser type) — no advertising profiles are built

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for purposes directly related to your booking and our service:

  • Confirming and managing your ride reservation
  • Sending SMS messages: booking confirmations, payment links, ride-day reminders, and status updates
  • Processing secure payment through Stripe
  • Communicating schedule changes or service updates relevant to your booking
  • Improving the quality of our service and our AI concierge, Amirah
  • Complying with legal and tax obligations (we are required to retain booking records)

SMS Communications

SMS Opt-In Disclosure

By providing your phone number on our booking forms, completing a booking conversation with Amirah (our AI concierge), or otherwise providing your phone number to us, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Synergy Lux Limo DFW LLC at the number provided.

Types of messages you may receive:

  • Booking confirmations and reservation details
  • Secure payment links via Stripe
  • Ride-day reminders and chauffeur arrival notifications
  • Follow-up messages if your booking inquiry was not completed
  • Occasional service reminders relevant to a prior booking

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your booking activity. You will typically receive 2–5 messages per booking (confirmation, payment link, and ride-day reminder).

Carrier disclaimer: Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS will be sent.

Reply HELP to any SMS for assistance, or contact us directly at (646) 879-1391.

Your consent to receive SMS is not a condition of any purchase. You can book a ride by calling us directly at (646) 879-1391 or emailing us without providing SMS consent.

SMS Messaging Data & Consent

When you opt in to receive SMS text messages from Synergy Lux Limo DFW LLC, the consent you provide and the mobile phone number you submit are treated as protected information. The above excludes text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile phone numbers collected for SMS messaging are used solely for the transactional purposes you have consented to, including booking confirmations, ride status updates, and reservation reminders. You may revoke your consent at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. Reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your booking activity.

Categories of personal information we may share with service providers (such as payment processors and dispatch software) for service fulfillment do not include SMS opt-in data, which is held separately and is not shared with affiliates, marketers, or other third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law:

  • Booking records: 7 years — required for tax and legal compliance
  • Communication logs (SMS, chat): 2 years
  • SMS marketing preferences and opt-out records: Retained indefinitely to honor your preferences
  • Payment records: As required by Stripe and applicable tax law

When data is no longer needed, it is deleted from our systems and we request deletion from our service providers where applicable.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Correct your data: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time — no questions asked
  • Withdraw consent: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us directly

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the information in the section below. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand basic traffic patterns (pages visited, general location by country, device type). We do not use advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or third-party behavioral tracking tools.

We do not run Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, or any other ad network on this site. No advertising profile is built from your visit.

If you prefer, you can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our booking forms or communicate with us.
